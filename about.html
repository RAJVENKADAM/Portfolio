Introduction

My Story & Technology Journey

As an Information Technology undergraduate student at Knowledge Institute of Technology, my curiosity drives me to look beyond the standard academic curriculum. I view software engineering not as just writing lines of code, but as a journey of building systems that solve real-world problems.

My journey began with exploring core programming concepts, and I am currently focusing my efforts on sharpening my problem-solving skills through Java and Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA). Rather than claiming mastery, I consider myself an active, dedicated learner who enjoys diving deep into logic and backend fundamentals.

I believe that technology becomes truly valuable when we try to build and execute it ourselves. Driven by this thought, I started AMUX- not as a formal institute, but as a collaborative space where I can share what I learn with my peers, helping us grow and gain hands-on experience together.

Learning Philosophy

I believe technology learning requires constant iteration. My approach focuses on exploring core concepts together through hands-on project building and practical execution.

Career Vision

My goal is to join a forward-thinking product team as an early-career Product Engineer to contribute to real-world software solutions. I am committed to continuously strengthening my core fundamentals in Java and Data Structures while learning how to build efficient, user-focused systems. Through practical, hands-on building, I aim to grow into a developer who bridges clean code with impactful user experiences.
